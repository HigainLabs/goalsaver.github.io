# Privacy Policy  
**Last Updated:** November 20, 2025  
**Developed by:** HiGain Labs Tech Pvt. Ltd.

GoalSaver (“the App”) is a goal-tracking application created by **HiGain Labs Tech Pvt. Ltd.** (“we”, “our”, “us”).  
This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled when you use the App.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

**GoalSaver does *not* collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.**

We do **not** collect:

- Names  
- Emails  
- Contacts  
- Financial data  
- Goals or entries  
- Device identifiers  
- Location information  

Any analytics collected by the Apple App Store (such as crash logs or usage data) is handled **solely by Apple**, not by us.

---

## 2. Use of Information

Because we do **not** collect any data:

- We do not track you  
- We do not share data  
- We do not use data for ads  
- We do not sell data  
- We cannot access any data you enter in the App  

All your entries (goals, income, expenses, etc.) remain stored **only on your device**.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

The App uses **no third-party SDKs**, trackers, or external analytics tools.  
Only Apple’s built-in analytics may apply under Apple’s policies.

---

## 4. Data Security

All data stays entirely **on your device**.  
We cannot view, retrieve, modify, or erase it.

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time, especially when new features or services are added.  
Any updates will be posted on this page.
